Schedule what you save.

Turn your reading queue into time you can actually use.

ReadSlot is a Chrome extension that saves articles, videos, PDFs, documentation, and repositories, estimates their reading time, and suggests suitable blocks in Google Calendar.

Learn how it works View the source code

How it works

You remain in control of every event.

1

Save

Capture useful content in a private reading queue stored in your browser.

2

Choose

Compare explainable suggestions based on availability and your preferences.

3

Confirm

Review the details and explicitly approve an event before it is created.

Google Calendar access

Only what scheduling requires.

When you choose to connect Google Calendar, ReadSlot lists calendars you can use, checks free/busy intervals, and manages only the reading events you explicitly confirm.

ReadSlot does not operate a backend, sell personal data, display advertising, or collect analytics. Google access tokens are managed by Chrome and are not stored in ReadSlot's database.
